### US-003: Generate round assignments with one guesser
Status: in_progress
Depends on: US-001, US-002

Description:
As a player, I want fair random assignment of guesser and performer roles so each round feels different.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] Randomly choose exactly one guesser from the current players.
- [ ] Assign all remaining players to role cards, always including mandatory roles and then optional roles as needed.
- [ ] Render guesser and role prompt templates with token replacement.
- [ ] Example: for 6 players, 1 guesser is selected and 5 performers receive roles where all mandatory roles appear exactly once.
- [ ] Negative case: if no compatible situation exists, round creation fails with a clear user-facing error.
